Good to know

A few common questions

What ages does Great Learners take?

We welcome children from 6 months to 5 years old, across our Baby Room, Toddlers and Pre-school classrooms.

Do you offer funded hours or accept childcare vouchers?

Yes — we accept childcare vouchers and offer the government's free entitlement for 3–4 year olds. Get in touch and we'll talk you through what applies to your family.

What are your opening hours?

We're open 8:00am to 6:00pm, Monday to Friday, 48 weeks of the year.

Is Great Learners part of a bigger group?

Great Learners is one of three Learners Montessori nurseries, alongside sister nurseries Budding Learners in Kingsbury and Little Learners in Edgware — each run locally under the same Montessori approach. This site covers our Golders Green branch only; the other branches have their own contact details.

What's your Ofsted rating?

Outstanding, following our inspection on 25 April 2023. Read the full report on Ofsted's site →
